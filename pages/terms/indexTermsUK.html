JUDGEMENT PROCESS

An internal judging panel from all departments in Vision Direct will select the best 20 jingles, and shortlisted entries will be uploaded to the Vision Direct YouTube channel on the 20th of May at 17:00.

The public can then help to get the Entrant extra points and increase their chance of winning by sharing and liking the Entrant's jingle online.This process will be until the 26th of May at 15:00.

On the 28th of May, a judging panel of experts from the radio and music industry will then select the final winner based on melody, composition, originality, lyrics (when applicable) and popularity (from the YouTube likes). The winner will be announced on the 1st of June on visiondirect.co.uk/jingle, visiondirect.es/jingle or visiondirect.nl/jingle.

Songs are judged equally on melody, composition, originality, popularity and lyrics (when applicable). All prizes will be awarded to the name as it appears on the entry form, and division of prizes among co-authors is the responsibility of winners. Entrant agrees to accept the final decision of Vision Direct and its judges.
